OUR ENGAGEMENT MODELS

As your technology partner, Ciklum offers a full freedom in choosing the suitable engagement model to your business needs. We have developed five engagement offerings, striking the balance between your expectations of involvement, timeliness and price.

    • EXTENDED TEAM

    Think of a strong, competent set of software engineers, QA specialists, and project managers, capable of implementing your project, anticipating your needs and delivering on schedule.

    Ciklum’s Extended Team is your own IT team mirrored in Spain, Poland, Ukraine, Belarus or Pakistan, and completely aligned with your strategic business goals.

    Discover now

    • SUPERVISED QUALITY

    In addition to the Extended Team model, we provide certified QA consultants to further cut costs and reduce time-to-market for your software development.

    With Supervised Quality, we assess your team processes and provide expert guidance in developing a QA strategy. It maximises team efficiency, improves planning, and code structuring.

    Discover now

    • MANAGED TEAM

    Managing the performance of individuals and your overall team in developing software is demanding, time-consuming and can distract from strategic goals. With Ciklum’s Managed Team, you focus on your business strategy, while we generate a high-performance culture and operational environment inside your team.

    With the Managed Team model, you tell us what the project backlog and requirements are, and we work through the backlog and get things done on schedule.

    Discover now

    • MANAGED PROJECT

    In every software development project, finding the right team members and choosing the relevant technologies are essential to success. Wrong choices at any stage can result in over budgeting and failed deadlines.

    With Managed Project you hand over the challenges of project development to Ciklum. We deliver your project according to the concepts and requirements defined by you.

    Discover now

    • MANAGED PRODUCT

    Instead of hiring the technology specialists and building your product from scratch, use Ciklum’s expertise and technical ecosystem to make your idea feasible and commercially available in the shortest time.

    With the Managed Product solution you don’t need to tell Ciklum what to do. Whether you need a feasibility study, proof of concept or finalised product ready for market launch – give us the concept and we’ll do the rest for you.

    Discover now
